Soundgasm - Rema

“Soundgasm” by Rema is not just a song about intimacy, it is a bold exploration of desire and passion in urban environments, pushing boundaries and challenging societal norms. It fearlessly delves into a topic often considered taboo, daring to articulate the nuances of romantic relationships and sexual experiences. In an unapologetic manner, Rema highlights the contemporary expressions of intimacy in African cities, providing a raw and honest portrayal of love, desire, and connection. His lyrics, such as "make sure that your daddy is not home, make sure that your mummy is not home," reveal the delicate balance between personal desires and cultural expectations, showcasing the deep respect for parental authority even in matters of intimacy. Through its daring approach and provocative lyrics, "Soundgasm" confronts societal taboos and opens up conversations about the complexities of intimacy in modern African society.
