Harmonizing Urban Narratives: Music's Role in African Cityscapes

To contextualize our exploration, we delve into the rich history and diverse experiences of African cities, where the sounds of music have played a profound role in shaping urban identities. Over time, as these cities have evolved, the changing soundscape of music has mirrored shifts in social, economic, and political dynamics. Through the analyses of different musics from the different African cities, we would see how music actively shapes perceptions of space, constructs identities, and fosters social cohesion within African cities.
